01 Introduction

标题 Structure Extraction from Texture via Relative Total Variation(基于总变差模型的纹理图像中图像主结构的提取方法)
关键词
作者 Lei Zhu etc.
发表会议名称 IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics
发表时间 21 December 2018
下载链接 sci-hub插件下载
阅读时间 2020-11-28

02 Abstract

　　Texture smoothing aims to smooth out textures in images, while retaining the prominent structures. This paper presents a saliency-aware approach to the problem with two key contributions. First, we design a deep saliency network with guided non-local blocks (GNLBs) for learning long-range pixel dependencies by taking the predicted saliency map at former layer as the guidance image to help suppress the non saliency regions in the shallow layer. The GNLB computes the saliency response at a position by a weighted
sum of features at all positions, and enables us to produce results that outperform existing deep saliency models. Second, we formulate a joint optimization framework to take saliency information when iteratively separating textures from structures: on the texture layer, we smooth out structures with the help of the saliency information and migrate structures from the texture to structure layer, while on the structure layer, we adopt another deep model to detect edges and simultaneous sparse coding to push textures back to the texture layer. We tested our method on a rich variety of images and compared it with several state-of-the-art methods. Both visual andquantitative comparison results show that our method better preserves structures while removing the texture components.

　　纹理平滑旨在平滑图像中的纹理，同时保留突出的结构。本文提出了一个针对问题的显着性方法有两个关键作用。 首先，我们设计了一个带有非本地引导的深度显著网络(GNLBs)，是通过将前一层的预测显显著图作为引导图像来学习远距离像素的相关性，有助于抑制浅层中的非显著区域。GNLB通过加权计算位置的显著响应所有位置上的特征的总和，能够产生优于现有深度显著模型的结果。其次，我们制定联合优化框架，以在从结构中迭代分离纹理时获取显著信息：在纹理层上，我们借助显著信息对结构进行平滑处理，并将结构从纹理层迁移到结构层，而
在结构层上，我们采用另一个深度模型来检测边缘，并同时进行稀疏编码以将纹理推回纹理层。我们在多种图像上测试了我们的方法，并将其与几种最新方法进行了比较。从定性和定量比较表明，我们的方法可以在去除纹理的同时能够更好地保留结构。

03 Conclusion

　　This paper presents a novel saliency-aware texture smoothing method by seamlessly integrating saliency information into a joint optimization framework. We first present a novel deep saliency model equipped with guided non-local blocks(GNLBs) to accurately detect the saliency regions of the input images. The GNLB is capable of learning spatial nonlocal features to allow similar but distant pixels to contribute to one pixel’s saliency response, while existing deep saliency models mainly exploit local neighborhood at each convolution cycle to detect saliency regions. Moreover, the GNLB incorporates the predicted saliency maps at deep layers into the non-local block as the guidance information to further suppress the non-saliency details at shallow layers. Experiments demonstrate the superiority of the presented network over existing methods in saliency detection. We then develop a joint optimization with three regularization terms to differently penalize structures and textures in various aspects. Extensive experiments demonstrate that the proposed method can better preserve structures while removing textures than existing methods, particularly the delicate yet salient structures, which can be easily captured
by human perception but are difficult to be preserved by previous methods. We also show the applicability of our texture smoothing method to enhance the results in other texture-related image editing applications such as the texture enhancement and seam carving.

　　本文通过将显著信息无缝集成到联合优化框架中，提出了一种新颖的显著性纹理平滑方法。我们首先提出一种新颖的深度显著模型，该模型配备有引导非局部块（GNLBs），以准确检测输入图像的显著区域。 GNLB能够学习空间非局部特征，以使相似但相距较远的像素能够为一个像素的显著响应做出贡献，而现有的深度显着性模型主要在每个卷积周期利用局部邻域来检测显著区域。此外，GNLB将深层的预测显著图合并到非本地块中作为引导信息，以进一步抑制浅层的非显著细节。实验证明了所提出的网络在显著检测方面优于现有方法。然后，我们使用三个正则项开发联合优化框架，以在各个方面对结构和纹理进行不同程度的。大量的实验表明，与现有方法相比，所提出的方法可以在去除纹理的同时更好地保留结构，尤其是精致而突出的结构，这些结构很容易被人的感知所捕获，但是很难被先前的方法所保留。我们还展示了我们的纹理平滑方法在其他纹理相关的图像编辑应用程序（例如纹理增强和接缝雕刻）中增强结果的适用性。

a. JOINT OPTIMIZATION(texture smoothing)

能量函数：$\min {\mathbf{S}, \mathbf{T}}|\mathbf{I}-\mathbf{S}-\mathbf{T}|{F}^{2}+\lambda{g} \mathcal{R}{\mathbf{g}}(\mathbf{S})+\lambda{e} \mathcal{R}{\mathbf{e}}(\mathbf{S})+\lambda{s} \mathcal{R}{\mathbf{s}}(\mathbf{T})\quad (5)$

  • I：输入图像，texture smoothing的目标是将输入图像分解为结构层和纹理层
  • S：结构层(我们想要的输出)
  • T：纹理层
  • 从这个目标函数看，本质上是一个迭代过程，将纹理从S推到T，将结构从T推到S
  • $||\mathbf{I}-\mathbf{S}-\mathbf{T}|_{F}^{2}$：是一个弗罗贝尼乌斯范数，用于约束输入和结果基本保真度项
  • $\lambda{g},\lambda{e},\lambda_{s}$：权重
  • $\mathcal{R}{\mathbf{g}}(\mathbf{S}),\mathcal{R}{\mathbf{e}}(\mathbf{S}),\mathcal{R}_{\mathbf{s}}(\mathbf{T})$：是作用于S和T的三个正则化项
  • $\mathcal{R}_{\mathbf{g}}(\mathbf{S})$：用于在S中平滑纹理
  • $\mathcal{R}_{\mathbf{e}}(\mathbf{S})$：用于在S中保留更多的结构
  • $\mathcal{R}_{\mathbf{s}}(\mathbf{T})$：利用区域显著性信息抑制T中的结构细节，并将结构推回S中

GSM-based sparsity prior(高斯尺度混合(GSM)模型) 公式(6)：$\begin{aligned} \mathcal{R}{\mathbf{g}}(\mathbf{S})=&|\mathbf{S}-\mathbf{D} \circ \boldsymbol{\alpha}|{F}^{2}+\sigma{n}^{2} \sum{p \in \mathbf{S}} \frac{\alpha{p}-\mu{p}}{\theta{p}} +4 \sigma{n}^{2} \log (\boldsymbol{\theta}+\varepsilon) \end{aligned} \quad (6)$

  • $\alpha_{p}$：S中以像素p为中心的图像patch的稀疏编码
  • $\boldsymbol{\alpha}$：串接所有$\alpha_{p}$的向量，将$\boldsymbol{\alpha}$的概率分布作为GSM模型
  • $\mathbf{D}$：一个字典
  • $\mathbf{D} \circ \boldsymbol{\alpha}$：从$\mathbf{D}$和$\boldsymbol{\alpha}$重构图像结构
  • $|\mathbf{S}-\mathbf{D} \circ \boldsymbol{\alpha}|_{F}^{2}$：使重建图像与结构层S相似
  • $\sigma_{n}^{2}$：基于输入图像纹理强度的参数
  • $\theta{p}$：$\alpha{p}$的标准推导
  • $\boldsymbol{\theta}$：串接所有的$\theta_{p}$向量
  • $\mu_{p}$：与在像素p处的patch关联的非局部最相似patch的稀疏编码的加权平均
  • $\varepsilon$：数值稳定的小正数
  • $\sigma{n}^{2} \sum{p \in \mathbf{S}} \frac{\alpha{p}-\mu{p}}{\theta{p}} +4 \sigma{n}^{2} \log (\boldsymbol{\theta}+\varepsilon) $：最大后验概率

Edge prior 公式(7)：$\mathcal{R}{\mathbf{e}}(\mathbf{S})=\sum{p \in \mathbf{S}}\left|\mathbf{\Omega}{p}(\mathbf{S})+\varepsilon{1}\right|^{-1}\quad (7)$

  • HED：基于深度学习的边缘检测器
  • $\mathbf{\Omega}(\mathbf{S})$：利用HED算法对结构层S进行检测到的边缘map
  • $\varepsilon_{1}$：一个常数，避免分母为0

Saliency prior 公式(8)： $\mathcal{R}{\mathbf{s}}(\mathbf{T})=\sum{p \in \mathbf{T}}\left{\left|\boldsymbol{\Psi}{p}(\mathbf{I})\right|^{\gamma}\left(\partial{x} \mathbf{T}{p}\right)^{2}+\left|\boldsymbol{\Psi}{p}(\mathbf{I})\right|^{\gamma}\left(\partial{y} \mathbf{T}{p}\right)^{2}\right}\quad (8)$

  • $\left(\partial{x} \mathbf{T}{p}\right)^{2}$：用于在水平方向上平滑的在像素p处的二次x分量偏导数
  • $\left(\partial{y} \mathbf{T}{p}\right)^{2}$：用于在垂直方向上平滑的在像素p处的二次y分量偏导数
  • ${\Psi}(\mathbf{I})$：表示检测到的显著性映射
  • ${\Psi}{p}(\mathbf{I})$：确定T中的像素p在$\left(\partial{x} \mathbf{T}{p}\right)^{2}$和$\left(\partial{y} \mathbf{T}_{p}\right)^{2}$上的平滑权重
  • $\gamma$：灵敏度参数
