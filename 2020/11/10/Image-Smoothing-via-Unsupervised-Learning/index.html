Created 2020-11-10| Updated 2020-11-10|图形学

01 Introduction

标题 Image Smoothing via Unsupervised Learning(通过无监督学习的图像光滑)
关键词
作者 QINGNAN FAN、JIAOLONG YANG、DAVID WIPF、BAOQUAN CHEN、XIN TONG
发表会议名称 ACM Transactions on Graphics
发表时间 November 2018
下载链接 https://arxiv.org/pdf/1811.02804.pdf
阅读时间 2020-11-10

02 Abstract

　　Image smoothing represents a fundamental component of many disparate computer vision and graphics applications. In this paper, we present a unified unsupervised (label-free) learning framework that facilitates generating flexible and high-quality smoothing effects by directly learning from data using deep convolutional neural networks (CNNs). The heart of the design is the training signal as a novel energy function that includes an edge-preserving regularizer which helps maintain important yet potentially vulnerable image structures, and a spatially-adaptive Lp flattening criterion which imposes different forms of regularization onto different image regions for better smoothing quality. We implement a diverse set of image smoothing solutions employing the unified framework targeting various applications such as, image abstraction, pencil sketching, detail enhancement, texture removal and content-aware image manipulation, and obtain results comparable with or better than previous methods. Moreover, our method is extremely fast with a modern GPU (e.g, 200 fps for 1280×720 images). Our codes and model are released in https://github.com/fqnchina/ImageSmoothing.

　　图像平滑是许多不同的计算机视觉和图形应用程序的基本组成部分。在本文中，我们提出了一个统一的无监督（无标签）学习框架，该框架可通过使用深度卷积神经网络（CNN）直接从数据中学习来促进产生灵活且高质量的平滑效果。设计的核心是训练信号，它是一种新颖的能量函数，其中包括一个边缘保持正则化器，有助于保持重要且易损的图像结构；以及一种空间自适应Lp展平准则，该准则将不同形式的正则化强加于不同的图像区域，以产生更好的平滑质量。我们采用针对各种应用程序（例如，图像抽象，铅笔素描，细节增强，纹理去除和内容感知图像处理）的统一框架，实现了一系列图像平滑解决方案，并获得了与以前的方法相当或更好的结果。此外，我们的方法使用现代GPU时非常快（例如，对于1280×720图像，为200 fps）。我们的代码和模型在https://github.com/fqnchina/ImageSmoothing中发布。

03 Conclusion

　　In this paper, we have presented an unsupervised learning approach for the task of image smoothing. We introduced a novel image smoothing objective function built upon the mixture of a spatiallyadaptive Lp flattening criterion and an edge-preserving regularizer. These criteria not only lead to state-of-the-art smoothing effects as demonstrated in our experiments, but also grant our method the flexibility to obtain different smoothing effects within a single framework. We have also shown that training a deep neural network on a large corpus of raw images without ground truth labels can adequately solve the underlying minimization problem and generate impressive results. Moreover, the end-to-end mapping from a single input image to its corresponding smoothed counterpart by the neural network can be computed efficiently on both GPU and CPU, and the experiments have shown that our method runs orders of magnitude faster than traditional methods. We foresee a wide range of applications that can benefit from our new pipeline.

　　8.1 Limitations and Future work　Our algorithm relies on some additional information to optimize the objective function during training, such as the detected structures or textures. Currently we employ some simple heuristic methods to detect the structures, and imperfect detection can influence the smoothing results. Figure 14 shows an example where our algorithm fails to extract some detailed textures perfectly. This issue can mitigated by introducing moderate effort of human interaction for refining the structure maps of the training data, or by synthesizing training images with separate textures and clear images similar to [Lu et al. 2018]. Developing more advanced detection algorithms is also one of our future works.

　　Due to the adaptively changed and spatially variant Lp flattening term and extra input information required for optimization, the optimization is complex and very challenging for traditional numerical solvers. To our knowledge, this is the first attempt of treating deep network as a numerical solver in the image smoothing field. In the future, we also would like to explore more complex and different tasks, such as multi-image or video processing.

　　在本文中，我们提出了一种用于图像平滑任务的无监督学习方法。我们介绍了一种新的图像平滑目标函数，该函数基于空间自适应Lp展平标准和边缘保留正则化。这些标准不仅可以带来实验中证明的最先进的平滑效果，而且可以使我们的方法具有在单个框架内获得不同平滑效果的灵活性。我们还表明，在没有标签的情况下，在大量原始图像上训练深度神经网络可以充分解决潜在的最小化问题并产生令人印象深刻的结果。此外，可以在GPU和CPU上高效地计算出神经网络从单个输入图像到其对应的平滑对应对象的端到端映射，并且实验表明，我们的方法比传统方法运行速度快了几个数量级。我们预见到很多应用可以从我们的新方法中受益。

　　8.1 局限性和未来工作　我们的算法依靠一些附加信息来优化训练过程中的目标函数，例如检测到的结构或纹理。 目前，我们采用一些简单的启发式方法来检测结构，而不完善的检测会影响平滑效果。 图14显示了一个示例，其中我们的算法无法完美地提取一些详细的纹理。 通过引入人为干预来改进训练数据的结构图，或通过合成具有单独纹理和清晰图像的训练图像[类似于Lu等人的方法,2018]，可以缓解此问题。 开发更高级的检测算法也是我们未来的工作之一。

　　由于Lp平坦化项的适应性变化和空间变化以及优化所需的额外输入信息，因此优化对于传统的数值求解器而言非常复杂且极具挑战性。 据我们所知，这是将深层网络视为图像平滑领域中的数值求解器的首次尝试。 将来，我们还希望探索更复杂和不同的任务，例如多图像或视频处理。
